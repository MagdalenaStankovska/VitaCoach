**Important Considerations:**

*   **Listen to your body:** If something hurts, stop! Adjust the intensity or duration as needed.
*   **Warm-up:** Begin each workout with 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking) and dynamic stretching (arm circles, leg swings).
*   **Cool-down:** End each workout with 5-10 minutes of static stretching, holding each stretch for 20-30 seconds.
*   **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water throughout the day, especially before, during, and after workouts.

**Workout Plan:**

**Day 1: Moderate Intensity Aerobic Activity**

*   **Activity:** Brisk Walking
*   **Duration:** 30 minutes
*   **Intensity:** Aim for a pace where you can hold a conversation but are slightly out of breath.

**Day 2: Muscle Strengthening**

*   **Exercises:**
    *   Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    *   Push-ups (against a wall or on your knees if needed): 3 sets of as many reps as possible (AMRAP) with good form.
    *   Plank: 3 sets, hold for 30 seconds
    *   Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps

**Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery**

*   **Activity:** Gentle stretching, light walk, or yoga.
*   **Duration:** 20-30 minutes

**Day 4: Moderate Intensity Aerobic Activity**

*   **Activity:** Cycling (stationary or outdoors)
*   **Duration:** 30 minutes
*   **Intensity:** Moderate, like day 1

**Day 5: Muscle Strengthening**

*   **Exercises:**
    *   Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
    *   Modified Plank (on knees): 3 sets, hold for 30 seconds
    *   Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps
    *   Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps

**Day 6: Vigorous Intensity Activity**

*   **Activity:** Jogging
*   **Duration:** 25 minutes
*   **Intensity:** Aim for a pace where you have a hard time holding a conversation

**Day 7: Rest**

**Progression:**

*   **Aerobic Activity:** Gradually increase the duration or intensity of your aerobic activities each week. Try adding 5-10 minutes to your walking or cycling sessions, or try jogging for short intervals during your walks.
*   **Muscle Strengthening:** As you get stronger, you can increase the number of repetitions, sets, or try more challenging variations of the exercises. For example, progress to full push-ups, deeper squats, or add light weights.

**Important Notes for Petar:**

*   **Consistency is Key:** Aim to stick to this plan as consistently as possible. Even if you miss a day, don't get discouraged, just get back on track the next day.
*   **Listen to Your Body:** This is very important! Don't push yourself too hard, especially in the beginning. Rest when you need to, and don't hesitate to modify exercises if they cause pain.
*   **Have Fun!** Exercise shouldn't feel like a chore. Find activities you enjoy, and you'll be more likely to stick with them.
