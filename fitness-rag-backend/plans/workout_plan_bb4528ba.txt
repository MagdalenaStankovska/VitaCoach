Okay, here's a 7-day ab-focused workout plan for a beginner, using only the exercises provided in the context. Remember to listen to your body and adjust as needed. Warm-up before each workout and cool down afterward with some light stretching.

**Important Considerations:**

*   **Beginner Level:** This program is designed for someone new to exercise.
*   **Consistency is Key:** Aim to stick to the plan as closely as possible, but don't be discouraged if you miss a day. Just get back on track the next day.
*   **Proper Form:** Focus on proper form over the number of repetitions. If you're unsure about the correct form, search for reliable online videos or consult with a qualified fitness professional.
*   **Progression:** As you get stronger, you can gradually increase the number of reps, sets, or the difficulty of the exercises.

**7-Day Ab Workout Plan**

**Day 1: Abdominal Endurance**

*   **Bicycle Crunches:** 3 sets of 15 reps
*   **Reverse Crunches:** 3 sets of 10 reps
*   **Standing Oblique Crunch with Cable:** 3 sets of 12 reps per side
*   **Walking:** 30 minutes

**Day 2: Active Recovery**

*   **Walking:** 30 minutes (moderate intensity)
*   **Stretching** Focus on flexibility

**Day 3: Core Strength**

*   **Bicycle Crunches:** 3 sets of 15 reps
*   **Reverse Crunches:** 3 sets of 10 reps
*   **Hanging Leg Raises:** 3 sets of as many reps as possible.
*   **Walking:** 30 minutes

**Day 4: Rest or Light Activity**

*   **Walking:** 20 minutes (light intensity, optional)
*   **Stretching**

**Day 5: Oblique Focus**

*   **Standing Oblique Crunch with Cable:** 3 sets of 15 reps per side
*   **Bicycle Crunches:** 3 sets of 15 reps
*   **Reverse Crunches:** 3 sets of 10 reps
*   **Walking:** 30 minutes

**Day 6: Active Recovery**

*   **Walking:** 30 minutes (moderate intensity)
*   **Stretching** Focus on flexibility

**Day 7: Rest**

*   Allow your body to fully recover. Light stretching is okay if desired.

**Important Notes:**

*   **Warm-up (5-10 minutes before each workout):** Light cardio, such as walking, and dynamic stretching.
*   **Cool-down (5-10 minutes after each workout):** Static stretching, holding each stretch for 20-30 seconds.
*   **Rest:** Rest for 30-60 seconds between sets.
*   **Hydration:** Drink plenty of water throughout the day, especially before, during, and after workouts.
*   **Nutrition:** Eat a balanced diet with adequate protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats to support your activity level.
*   **Progression:** When exercises become too easy, gradually increase the number of repetitions or sets, or slightly increase the intensity.

Remember to consult your doctor or a qualified healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program.
